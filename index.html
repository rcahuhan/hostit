root orderNumber
Type: string

an order number is a unique identifier assigned to each individual purchase made by a customer. This number is typically generated by the ecommerce platform or system and is used to track and manage the order throughout the entire fulfillment process, from purchase to delivery. The order number is often included in order confirmation emails and invoices, and is used as a reference by both the customer and the seller for any order-related inquiries or issues

Must be at least 4 characters long

Must be at most 100 characters long
